备注1：
catkin_make报错如下：
fatal error: Eigen/Geometry: 没有那个文件或目录
   46 | #include <Eigen/Geometry>
解决：
sudo ln -sf /usr/include/eigen3/Eigen/ /usr/include/Eigen
sudo ln -sf /usr/include/eigen3/unsupported/ /usr/include/unsupported

备注2：
catkin_make报错如下：
make[2]: *** [aubo_robot/aubo_driver/CMakeFiles/aubo_driver.dir/build.make:101：/home/hy/aubo/devel/lib/aubo_driver/aubo_driver] 错误 1
make[1]: *** [CMakeFiles/Makefile2:4761：aubo_robot/aubo_driver/CMakeFiles/aubo_driver.dir/all] 错误 2
make: *** [Makefile:141：all] 错误 2
Invoking "make -j6 -l6" failed
解决：
wget http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/main/p/protobuf/libprotobuf9v5_2.6.1-1.3_amd64.deb
sudo dpkg -i libprotobuf9v5_2.6.1-1.3_amd64.deb
